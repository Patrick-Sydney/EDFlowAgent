---

# 🖼️ UX Flow

1. Tap **Set Age** → numeric pad drops down **inline** right under the button.
2. Tap digits → they append to the read-only Age input.

   * e.g., for “45”: tap `4` then `5`.
3. Tap **Confirm** → pad disappears, Age stays in the field.
4. Tap **Clear** if mistake.

---

# ✅ Benefits

* **Inline UX** — pad lives right where the user clicked, no hunting on screen.
* **Efficient** — max 4 taps to set an age.
* **Safe** — prevents accidental entry into a free-text field (age stays numeric-only).
* **Consistent** — same drawer sizing/spacing as Sex buttons and ATS buttons.
